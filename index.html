How to install my Datapacks

1

Datapack

Save the downloaded Datapack.zip to .minecraft/saves/yourworld/datapacks

You open your Datapack folder by clicking in Minecraft on: "Singleplayer/yourworld/Edit/Open World Folder" and opening the "datapacks" folder
2

Resourcepack

Save the downloaded Resourcepack.zip to .minecraft\resourcepacks

You open your Resourcepack folder by clicking in Minecraft on: "Options/Resource Packs/Open Pack Folder"

3

Activate

In Minecraft, go to "Settings/Resource Packs", select the appropriate Resourcepack and activate it. When you're in your world, do /reload
